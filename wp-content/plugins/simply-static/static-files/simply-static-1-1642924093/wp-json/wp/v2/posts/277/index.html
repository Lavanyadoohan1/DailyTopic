Ananya Panday is currently preparing for her upcoming film Gehraiyaan, in which she will co-star with Deepika Padukone, Siddhant Chaturvedi, and Dhairya Karwa. For the film’s promotion, the Gen-Z fashionista wore a trendy bralette and faux leather pants, which set the internet on fire.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Ananya took to Instagram today to reveal her Gehraiyaan promotional look in a series of photos. “#Gehraiyaan promotions legggggoooo,” she captioned the post. “The trailer is now available.”<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The bralette-styled top was paired with chocolate brown faux leather straight-fit pants with front pleats, a loose silhouette, a high-rise waistline, and ankle-length cuffs. She completed her look with animal-print above-the-ankle high-heeled boots.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In ‘Gehraiyaan,’ Ananya will be seen romancing Siddhant Chaturvedi for the first time. The film’s trailer depicted the actress in a new light as a performer, and her fans are eagerly awaiting its release.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
