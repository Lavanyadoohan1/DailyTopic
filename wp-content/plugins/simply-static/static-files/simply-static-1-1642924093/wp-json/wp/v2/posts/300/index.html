The first images of Etihad Rails are now available. These images depict the design and appearance of the passenger trains operated by Ethiad Rail in the United Arab Emirates. The carriages on these trains are silver and grey with a red Etihad Rail logo. These images also show the interiors, providing the first look at what these trains will be like on the inside.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

According to reports, these trains will connect 11 cities and areas across the UAE, from Al Sila (in the west) to Fujairah (northern part). The trains will travel at a speed of 200 kmph between these points, seating 400 people inside the carriages.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

According to Etihad Rail, the stations will be designed to celebrate regional traditions. Additionally, the idea is to build some of the stations in cities, though the location is not yet finalized. \u201cWe see the stations as urban centers where people can gather,\u201d said Ahmed Al Hashemi, executive director of Etihad Rail passengers, in a statement.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

These trains carriages will be equipped with Wi-Fi, charging stations, entertainment systems, and food and beverages for everyone in the UAE. In addition, for convenience, the rail system will be integrated with other modes of public transportation.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The train is expected to take fifty minutes to travel from Abu Dhabi to Dubai and around 100 minutes to travel from Abu Dhabi to Fujairah. The entire system is expected to generate Dh 23 billion in profits and provide approximately 9,000 jobs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
