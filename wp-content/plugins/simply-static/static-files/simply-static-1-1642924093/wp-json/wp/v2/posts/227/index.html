COVID-19 infections increased by 2,073 from the previous day.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

A total of 42,391 patients were discharged, bringing the total number of recovered patients to 70,09,823.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

According to Maharashtra Health Minister Rajesh Tope, despite a high positive rate, at least 95% of beds are vacant or hospitalization is lower than in the second wave of COVID-19.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

“In the state, 95% of the beds are vacant,” he said. Approximately 4-5% of patients are admitted to hospitals for treatment.”<\/p>\n\n\n\n
