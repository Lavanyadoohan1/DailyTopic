Gucci, an Italian luxury fashion house, has come under fire on social media for using real tigers in their advertising campaign. The Gucci Tiger Collection debuted earlier this month to commemorate the Chinese Year of the Tiger, which begins on February 1st. Many animal rights activists, including the founder of Big Cat Rescue, Carole Baskin, and PETA, were outraged by their decision to use real tigers for the shoot.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
