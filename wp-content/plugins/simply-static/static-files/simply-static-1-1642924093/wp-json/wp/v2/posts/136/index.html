Cristiano Ronaldo voted for Robert Lewandowski to win the Best FIFA Men’s Player Award, while Lionel Messi supported Neymar of Paris Saint-Germain.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Lewandowski, of Bayern Munich and Poland, was named the champion for the second time in succession on Monday, beating out Messi and Liverpool striker Mohamed Salah.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Lewandowski won 48 points after receiving votes from national team captains, coaches, journalists, and fans, while Messi obtained 44 points and Salah received 39 points.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Who did Ronaldo cast his vote for?<\/strong>
As captain of Portugal, Ronaldo was permitted to vote for his chosen contender to win the title.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The Manchester United midfielder handed Lewandowski his first vote, while Chelsea midfielders N’Golo Kante and Jorginho received their second and third.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Messi voted for whom?<\/strong>
Argentina captain Messi did not vote for any of the other finalists to win the award.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Instead, he gave Neymar his first vote, and his second to another PSG player, Kylian Mbappe.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Meanwhile, Messi has named Real Madrid striker Karim Benzema as his third favorite to win the World Player of the Year award in 2021.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Who did Lewandowski cast his vote for?<\/strong>
When it came to choosing his favorite player, the eventual winner did not vote for Messi or Ronaldo.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
